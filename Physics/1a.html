Why is it more difficult to revolve a stone by tying it to a longer string than by tying it to a shorter string?

Ans: The torque I required to produce angular acceleration & is given by t = I a, (I = m r^2) is the moment of inertia. More is the distance of the stone from the axis of rotation, greater is its moment of inertia and hence more torque is required to produce same angular acceleration. Therefore, it is more difficult to revolve a stone by tying it to a longer string than by tying it to a shorter string

Explain, why the speed of a whirl winds in a tornado is alarmingly high.

Ans: The air from the nearby regions gets concentrated in a small space inside a whirl wind. As a result the value of moment of inertia decreases by a large amount. From the law of conservation of angular momentum due to decrease in moment of inertia. th angular velocity increases alarmingly and hence the speed of a whirl wind in a tornado is alarmingly high.

The cap of a bottle can be easily opened with the help of two fingers than with one finger. Why?

Ans: When we apply the force by two fingers to open the cap of a bottle, a moment of couple i.e. torque is produced which helps to open it easily. When we apply the force by a single finger, it cannot produce torque or turning effect or produce less turning effect and hence, be difficult to open cap of bottle.

A fan with blades takes longer time to come to rest than without the blades. Why?

Ans: Since moment of inertia of an object depends on its mass, the object which has higher mass has higher moment of inertin The object having higher moment of inertia takes longer time to come to rest. Here fan with blade has higher moment of inertia then without blade. Hence it takes longer time to come to rest .

Why in hand driven grinding machine, handle is put near the circumference of the stone or wheel?

Can a single force applied to a body change both its translational and rotational motion? Explain.

Ans: Yes, when a force is applied to a body such that the body rolls without slipping on a horizontal plane surface, it rotates about the horizontal axis through the centre of mass and undergoes displacement in the forward direction. In other word, the body rotates about horizontal axis but at the same time the centre of mass of the body moves linearly forward. So, a single force applied to a body changes both its translational and rotational motion
